Archive Hoodie

A hoodie that combines archival style with modern jacquard fabric.

Luxurious materials add sophistication to authentic looks from the adidas archives. Bold attitude defines this women's hoodie. It's made of geometric-patterned jacquard fabric with contrast panels. A "Team adidas" graphic sits front and center on this relaxed-fit pullover.

Washing instructions
Machine wash cold delicate cycle
Do not bleach
Tumble dry low heat
Do not iron
Do not dry clean
Machine wash cold delicate cycle
Extra care information
  • Use mild detergent only
  • Wash with fasteners closed
  • Do not iron print
  • Wash and dry inside out
  • Wash with like colors

  • Half zip with drawcord-adjustable hood
  • Ribbed cuffs and hem
  • Rubber-print "Team adidas" graphic and embroidered Trefoil on chest
  • Relaxed fit
  • 100% polyester jacquard
